Bio

Kendrick Lamar is an American rapper from Compton, California, born June 17, 1987.

When Kendrick was 8, he saw the video for "California Love" by Tupac and Dr. Dre and was inspired to become a rap artist.

When he was 16, he relased his first full length mixtape called Youngest Head Nigga in Charge under the stage name K-dot. This mixtape eventually led to a contract with Top Dawg Entertainment.

After releasing several mixtapes in the 2000's as an independent artist, Kendrick signed with Lil Wayne and soon dropped his stage name K-dot using his birth name instead.

In March 2012, Lamar signed a deal with Interscope Records and Aftermath Entertainment and relased 2 albums good kid, m.A.A.d City and ending his career as an indpendent artist.
